24 Hour Concierge

Our concierge staff are on hand 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Their services include: • Announcing visitors • Accepting and delivering packages • Providing directories of local services • Caring for your cats when you are away • Making restaurant and theater reservations • Arranging dog-walking service for your pets • Providing event information and arranging discount group tickets • Taking care of your plants during your absence • Arranging housekeeping services • Arranging for taxi and limousine service • Arranging laundry and dry-cleaning pickup and delivery

Welcome Pet Lovers!

At The Village four-legged members of your family, both cats and dogs, are welcomed. The Villages' proximity to green spaces-such as the large landscaped plaza between The Village entrance and the Convention Center, and the Loring Greenway, which begins at The Villages' back door and connects with Loring Park - and the pedestrian-friendly environs that surround it create and ideal setting for Residents who have dogs.

The Fitness and Health Center

Our gym floor is as spacious as they come. Enjoy scenic views from the cardio equipment. You will enjoy a state-of-the-art fitness center with health club-style locker rooms, complete with showers and saunas, a large heated indoor swimming pool and spa, complimentary yoga and exercise classes, and more. Come spring, you will enjoy our rooftop sundecks (which have personal lockers for your convenience), tennis and sport courts, private jogging track, nine-hole putting green and rooftop picnic area.
